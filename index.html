Get Your Questions Answered

Refinancing parent loans can be overwhelming. Don't worry. We've got you covered.



  • Can you Refinance Parent Plus Loans?
  • Yes! PenFed can refinance parent student loans of any type, and can even consolidate Parent PLUS loans with your private student loans.

  • Can you Consolidate Parent PLUS loans with direct loans?
  • With a PenFed Parent PLUS student loan refinance, you can consolidate Parent PLUS loans with other types of federal direct loans, including both subsidized and unsubsidized. Direct loan debt in the child’s name can be assumed by the parent, whether or not the child is included on the refinance application.

  • Are you eligible for loan forgiveness after a Parent PLUS refinance?
  • When you refinance Parent PLUS student loans, you unfortunately do lose the federal benefits associated with those loan programs. It is important to keep these benefits in mind if you are considering consolidating Parent PLUS loans.

  • Can a child refinance Parent PLUS loans?
  • Yes, a child who has graduated with at least a bachelors degree can use a PenFed Parent PLUS refinance to assume the debt into their own name. We can even help if the child is interested in consolidating Parent PLUS loans with their own federal and/or private loans.
